We Love Each Other

Our Story

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • First We Meet

    January 23, 2019

    Iqbal and Balqis' love story began one warm summer evening in a bustling cafe. Iqbal, a talented photographer immersed in his work, noticed Balqis, a witty and ambitious writer, lost in her novel. Their eyes met, igniting an inexplicable connection. Unable to resist the pull, Iqbal approached her with a shy smile, sparking a lively conversation about their shared interests. Little did they know that this ordinary encounter would lay the groundwork for an extraordinary journey of love and discovery.

  • First Date

    May 24, 2022

    The night of their first date was magical, and Iqbal and Balqis found themselves lost in each other's company. Balqis was touched by the gesture of the bouquet of her favorite flowers, and as they reached the rooftop restaurant, the city skyline shimmered under the starlit sky. The ambiance was perfect, allowing them to share stories, dreams, and aspirations freely. Their laughter and easy banter made it feel like they had known each other for a lifetime, and time seemed to slow down as they savored every moment together.

  • In A Relationship

    Jun 1, 2023

    As their relationship blossomed, Iqbal and Balqis found solace and strength in each other. They supported and encouraged one another in pursuing their passions, Iqbal with his photography and Balqis with her writing. Their love was the driving force behind their creativity, and their affection for each other only grew stronger with time. They cherished the simplest joys, from stolen kisses on busy streets to spontaneous picnics in the park, finding happiness in each other's presence. Their friends and family admired the genuine love and happiness radiating from the couple, and their story became an inspiration, a testament to the power of fate and the magic of true love. Together, they knew that their love story was destined to be written for eternity, with every chapter filled with love, laughter, and the beauty of finding a soulmate in the most unexpected places.
